Chicken Pizza

Indulge in the savory delight of our chicken pizza, where tender, seasoned chicken pieces rest atop a bed of tangy tomato sauce and melted mozzarella cheese, all nestled on a perfectly crisp pizza crust. Every bite is a harmony of flavors, making it a delicious choice for any pizza enthusiast.

Once baked to golden perfection in our brick oven, our chicken pizza emerges piping hot, with the cheese bubbly and slightly caramelized around the edges. Each slice offers a symphony of flavors and textures, from the crispy crust to the succulent chicken and gooey cheese, making it a delicious and satisfying choice for pizza lovers of all ages.

Chicken Pepperoni

Our chicken pepperoni pizza is a delicious twist on the classic favorite. We start with a handcrafted crust, topped with a robust tomato sauce that's been simmered with garlic, basil, and a hint of red pepper flakes for a touch of heat.

The result is a mouthwatering pizza that offers a tantalizing combination of flavors and textures, from the juicy chicken to the spicy pepperoni and melty cheese. It's a satisfying choice for anyone craving a delicious and hearty pizza experience.

Energy Drink which you can make at home.

In a pitcher or large glass, combine 2 cups of water, 1 tablespoon of honey or maple syrup for sweetness, 1/4 teaspoon of sea salt for electrolytes, and 1/4 cup of freshly squeezed lemon juice for flavor and vitamin C. Stir the mixture until the sweetener and salt are dissolved, then taste and adjust the sweetness or tartness if needed. Optionally, add a small amount of grated ginger or a pinch of cayenne pepper for an extra kick. Chill the homemade energy drink in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving. Serve over ice, if desired, and enjoy your refreshing and energizing homemade drink!

Fresh Veggie and rice combo for dinner.

For a wholesome dinner option, consider a delightful combination of fresh veggies and rice. Start by cooking brown rice until tender and fluffy. Meanwhile, in a skillet, heat olive oil and saut minced garlic and sliced onions until fragrant. Add a medley of julienned carrots, colorful bell peppers, broccoli florets, snap peas, and mushrooms, stir-frying until tender-crisp. Whisk together soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, and ginger to create a flavorful sauce, then pour it over the vegetables, allowing them to absorb the delicious flavors. Serve the vegetable stir-fry over the cooked brown rice, garnishing with sesame seeds and chopped green onions for added texture and freshness. This veggie and rice combo offers a nutritious, satisfying meal that's bursting with flavor and goodness, perfect for a wholesome dinner. Enjoy the vibrant colors and tastes of this homemade delight!
